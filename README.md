# 🏡 Airbnb Data Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Airbnb data to uncover meaningful insights about **customer behavior, pricing trends, city performance, and review patterns** using Power BI.

The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights that help in better decision-making for hosts, businesses, and stakeholders.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze Airbnb listings across multiple cities
* Identify the most preferred room types
* Understand pricing variations across property types
* Evaluate customer reviews and trust factors
* Study seasonal demand patterns

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Hosts:** 182,024
* **Total Listings:** 279,712
* **Available Bookings:** 115,607
* **Cities Covered:** 10
* **Property Types:** 144

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔹 Growth Trends

* Significant growth observed between 2012–2015
* Peak in listings occurred in 2015
* Decline started after 2016 due to regulations
* Sharp drop during COVID-19 period

### 🔹 Market Share by City

* Paris, New York, and Sydney contribute nearly half of total listings
* These cities also dominate total reviews

### 🔹 Pricing Analysis

* Hotel rooms are the most expensive (~$800)
* Entire homes average around ~$673
* Private rooms are more affordable (~$462)

### 🔹 Customer Preferences

* 65% of users prefer **Entire Place**
* Very low demand for shared and hotel rooms

### 🔹 Review Behavior

* 98.8% of users give only 1–3 reviews
* Indicates high participation but low repeat reviewing

### 🔹 Trust Factor

* Listings with profile pictures have higher trust and engagement
* Verified users contribute more reliable interactions

### 🔹 City Performance

* Best rated cities: Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro
* Lowest rated cities: Hong Kong and Istanbul

### 🔹 Seasonality

* Peak activity from April to August
* Increase in bookings during November–December (holiday season)
